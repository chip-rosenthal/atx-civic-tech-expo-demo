Open Austin and the City of Austin have been working together since 2009 to support new web and Internet technology in Austin.

Toward the end of 2011, we brought new focus to civic apps and data. We worked to open more city data for public use. We created new apps and websites.

Today, at the ATX Civic Tech Expo, we'd like to show you what we've been able to do, and give you a glimpse of some projects currently underway you may hear more about in coming months.
