What's a Hackathon?

The weekend kicks off with a project pitch session. Everybody who has a project idea will get three minutes to describe the challenge and what can be done. The hackers will vote for the project they most want to work on. The projects with the most support will be selected. The hackers will break up to work on their selected projects for a concerted two-day period. The goal is to produce a demonstrable prototype by the end of the event.
