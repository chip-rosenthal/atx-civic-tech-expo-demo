Open Austin is a local citizen volunteer group that advocates for open government, open data, and civic technology. We were founded in 2009 to support citizen interests in the City of Austin website and online presence.We are affiliated with Code for America, a national non-profit that uses technology to help governments work better, as the Civic Brigade in Austin.

Application Gallery

Community developed applications that use geospatial data and mapping technologies.

VoteATX

"Where is the best place to vote right now?"

Voters have numerous options and the best choice isn't always obvious – and may vary from day to day during the early voting period.

This mobile-friendly, responsive web app helps you find the best choices for polling locations.

Data provided by Travis County Clerk and Travis County Tax Assessor-Collector.

Metro Rappid

The "Metro Rappid" app connects to the real-time API for the Metro Rapid bus service, and provides a simple, optimized interface to bus routes and status.

The app was selected as the participants' favorite at the ATX Hack for Change 2014.

Our Initiatives

Hack

We're an active group of civic-minded hackers and creatives who build apps that solve civic problems. We also help local government leverage open source technology and encourage city government to open datasets for public access. We're the Code for America Brigade for Austin.

Gov

Open Austin focuses on ensuring that all local public sector agencies embrace open data and open government principles, provide adequate oversight over public information, and support civic software developers.
