Apps Developed by the City

From Code to America Brigades to hack-a-thons across Austin, independent civic application developers and City employees use public datasets to create cool apps, research important issues and visualize data in new and exciting ways.

The City has created a new app library to catalog the civic tools created by our employees and partners -- as well as the general public.
