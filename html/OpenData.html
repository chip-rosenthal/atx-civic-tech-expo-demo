Open Data

What do you get when you provide universal access to science and government data, pair it with modern web technology, and provide it to volunteer programmers who want to solve real-world problems? Enter Open Austin and other volunteer organizations like it. Open data is about increasing the availability of data sets and using that data to improve communities. For citizens, open data means greater government transparency, more convenient citizen-government interactions, and a more informed community. Open data empowers citizens to work with their government to improve the quality of life.

For the Community

Open data isn't just for hackers. Any citizen can be a data analyst, thanks to powerful visualization and reporting features built into the City of Austin data portal.
