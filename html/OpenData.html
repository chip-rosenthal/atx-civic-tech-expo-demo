Open Data

Across the nation -- at the federal, state and local levels -- government bodies are releasing their data for the public to freely use. They are embracing the growing open data movement.

There are many benefits to open data. It increases government transparency. It unlocks the social and commercial value contained within. It fosters citizen participation.
